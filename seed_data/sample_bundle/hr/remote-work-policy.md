# Remote Work Policy

## Scope

This policy applies to all full-time employees who wish to work remotely for part or all of their scheduled work week.

## Eligibility

Remote work is available to any employee whose role does not require physical presence. Department heads retain discretion to deny requests based on business needs.

## Core Hours

All remote employees must be available during **core hours** of 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM local time, Monday through Friday.

## Equipment

Employees are entitled to an [Equipment Stipend](./equipment-stipend.md) to set up their home office.

## Data Security

Remote workers must adhere to the [Data Protection Policy](../compliance/data-protection.md). Company-issued devices must use VPN when accessing internal systems.

## Approval

Requests must be submitted to the employee's direct manager and HR at least two weeks before the proposed start date.
