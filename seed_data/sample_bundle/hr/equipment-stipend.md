# Equipment Stipend Policy

## Amount

Each eligible employee may receive up to **$1,500 USD** per calendar year for home office equipment.

## Eligible Items

- Desk and chair
- Monitor and peripherals
- Webcam and microphone
- Networking equipment (router, switch)
- Ergonomic accessories

## Process

1. Obtain manager approval for the purchase.
2. Submit receipts via the expense management system.
3. Reimbursement is processed within 15 business days.

## Relationship to Other Policies

This stipend is part of the broader [Remote Work Policy](./remote-work-policy.md). Equipment purchased under this stipend remains company property for the duration of employment.
