# Code of Conduct

## Core Principles

1. **Integrity** — Act honestly and ethically in all business dealings.
2. **Respect** — Treat colleagues, clients, and partners with dignity.
3. **Accountability** — Take responsibility for your actions and decisions.

## Reporting Violations

Violations should be reported to HR or via the anonymous ethics hotline. Retaliation against reporters is strictly prohibited.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination, as outlined in applicable employment policies.
